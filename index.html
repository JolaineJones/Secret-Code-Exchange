Tiamoa's Lucky Elephants!

Elephants are considered a symbol of good luck, prosperity, and good fortune in many cultures and religions.

The upward-pointing trunks of elephant statues are regarded to be particularly lucky and bring wealth to the home.

Elephants can also symbolize:

Abundance: Elephants are associated with abundance, which is related to overall good fortune.

Protection: In ancient times, some cultures enlisted elephants in the war effort.

Strength: Elephants are strong animals that can weigh anywhere from 4,000 to 14,000 pounds.

Family and friendship: Elephants symbolize family and friendship through close bonds.

Wisdom: Elephants are smart, and their millions of years of survival bares this out.
